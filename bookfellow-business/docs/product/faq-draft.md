Collapsible list on the public site. **Not** a separate “What we support” page — that question is one dropout here.

| # | Question | Answer lean |
|---|----------|-------------|
| 1 | What is Bookfellow? | We want people to **read more** — and to **understand** and **reflect** on what they read. Companion for books you already own: enhance what you’re reading, recall the throughlines, dig deeper. Not a substitute. Voice: [voice-copy.md](voice-copy.md) · principle: [decision](../decisions/2026-07-22-guiding-principle-read-understand-reflect.md). |
| 2 | What books do you support? | Trade fiction + trade NF, English. Not every book (textbooks, comics/manga, self-pub/KDP, academic, picture books, etc.). Decline clearly; waitlist for class if support expands. |
| 3 | Is it free? | Yes at the floor: **1 companion free every 3 months for everyone** — because reading more (and understanding/reflecting) is why we exist. Membership adds credits (~$10/mo sketch). À la carte extras. |
| 4 | What if I cancel? | Keep unlocks for life of site; ~3 months if we fold. Not forever — Terms. Lose allotments + member price. |
| 5 | Is this like Blinkist? | No — we don’t replace the book with a summary. We push you back into the book. |
| 6 | I have a redemption / invite code | Create account → “Have a redemption code?” Existing members redeem in-app (no lecture on the form). |
| 7 | Where is Bookfellow available? | US-first; AU web unavailable today. |
