## Company why (must land on logged-out)

**Read more** · **understand** what you read · **reflect** on what you have read.  
That is why Bookfellow exists — and why **everyone gets 1 free companion every 3 months**. Punchy hooks below sit under this; they don’t replace it. Canon: [decisions/2026-07-22-guiding-principle-read-understand-reflect.md](../decisions/2026-07-22-guiding-principle-read-understand-reflect.md).

## Style

- **Short. Punchy.** One beat per line.
- Enhancement framing: books **you own** / already reading — not a substitute library.
- Prefer verbs that **upgrade, deepen, recall, understand, reflect** — not “AI summarizes for you.”
- Avoid founder names, “notify Brian,” Melbourne collision claims, absolute forever.

**Locked product tagline (still):** **companion for your books** — [north-star.md](../north-star.md). Punchy lines below are **hooks / subheads / How bullets**, not a replacement for that tagline unless Brian locks one as hero.
