# Guiding principle — read more · understand · reflect (2026-07-22)

**Status:** locked lean (Brian)  
**Surface:** company messaging — especially **logged-out** home / How / Pricing / FAQ

## Principle

Bookfellow exists because we want people to **read more**. We help them **understand** what they read, and we **encourage reflection** on what they have read.

That is why **everyone gets 1 free companion every 3 months** — not a promo gimmick; an expression of the principle. Paid membership adds more; the free floor stays for all tiers.

## Implications

| Area | What it means |
|------|----------------|
| Why we exist | More reading of books people already own / are consuming — not a substitute for reading |
| Product job | Understanding + reflection sit beside recall / return-to-book (thesis job pair) |
| Free floor | 1 / 3 months is **mission-backed**, not only monetization math |
| Logged-out copy | Public sites must carry this triad + the free floor as why, not bury it in Pricing fine print |
| Voice | Punchy hooks still OK; they should not contradict “read more / understand / reflect” |
