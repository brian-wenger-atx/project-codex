## Afterglow — who owns `.read`? Alt URLs (2026-07-21)

### `.read`

- **Registry / sponsoring org:** [Amazon Registry Services, Inc.](https://www.iana.org/domains/root/db/read.html) (IANA).
- **Technical RDAP:** Nominet (`rdap.nominet.uk/read/`).
- **Status:** Nominet lists `.read` under **“Registries which have not yet launched”** — Amazon operates it; **not open to public registration**.
- **Does Amazon “own all .read”?** Amazon controls the **entire TLD**. You cannot buy `anything.read` from a registrar today. Most second-level names (including `afterglow.read`) simply **do not exist** in the registry (RDAP 404) — that is *not* availability. Only Amazon can create `.read` names if/when they ever launch or use them internally. Same Amazon “not launched” bucket includes `.book`, `.author`, `.smile`, etc.
- Contrast: Amazon *has* launched other TLDs for public sale (e.g. `.bot`, `.now`, `.deal`) — `.read` is not among them.
