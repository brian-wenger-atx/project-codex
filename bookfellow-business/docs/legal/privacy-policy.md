## 1. Who we are

**Bookfellow** (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates **bookfellow.io** and related services. Bookfellow is a **companion for books you are reading or listening to** — it helps you remember, reflect, and return to titles you already own. It is not a substitute for reading or listening to the book itself.

Questions about this policy: **[support@bookfellow.io](mailto:support@bookfellow.io)**.

## 2. What this policy covers

This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you:

- visit **bookfellow.io**,
- contact us by email, or
- use Bookfellow products and features.

A separate **Terms of Service** governs your use of Bookfellow. If this policy conflicts with a signed agreement with you, that agreement controls.

## 3. Information we collect

### 3.1 Information you provide

Depending on what is available on the site, we may collect:

| What | When | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **Email address** | Waitlist sign-up, account registration, or when you email us | To respond, send product updates you opt into, and operate your account |
| **Name or display name** | If you choose to provide it | To personalize the service |
| **Messages you send us** | Support or other correspondence | To help you |
| **Account and profile details** | When accounts are available | To operate your library and preferences |
| **Reading progress and preferences** | When you mark progress or configure the app | To provide spoiler-safe, progress-bounded companion features |
| **Notes, reflections, and chat content** | When you use companion features | To store your content, improve your experience, and provide the service you request |
| **Proof of purchase** | If you redeem a publisher or partner offer | To verify eligibility for a title-specific companion (we do not need to host the book itself) |
| **Payment information** | When you subscribe or purchase | Processed by our **payment processor** — we do not store full card numbers on our servers |

### 3.2 Information collected automatically

When you use the site or app, we may automatically collect:

- **Device and browser information** (type, operating system, general settings)
- **Log data** (IP address, pages viewed, timestamps, referring URL, crash or error reports)
- **Cookies and similar technologies** (see Section 8)

We use this information to operate, secure, and improve Bookfellow.

### 3.3 Voice and images

If you use voice or image features:

- **Audio** you submit for transcription or conversation is processed to provide the feature. We **do not keep raw audio** after processing unless you explicitly save a recording (if that option exists).
- **Photos** you submit for text extraction (for example, a marked passage) are processed to extract text. We **do not keep the original image** after processing unless you choose to save it.

Extracted text may be stored as part of your notes or companion session, subject to your controls.

## 4. How we use information

We use information to:

- provide, maintain, and improve Bookfellow;
- create and deliver companion content bounded by **your marked progress** (spoiler-safe by design);
- process waitlist sign-ups, accounts, subscriptions, and purchases;
- communicate with you about the service, security, and lawful requests;
- detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and technical issues;
- comply with law and enforce our terms.

We may use **aggregated or de-identified** data that cannot reasonably identify you for analytics and product improvement.

## 5. How we share information

We **do not sell** your personal information.

We may share information with:

- **Service providers** who help us run Bookfellow (for example: hosting, email delivery, payment processing, customer support tools, and technology partners that process requests on our behalf). They may use information only to perform services for us, subject to contractual protections.
- **Professional advisors** (lawyers, accountants) under confidentiality obligations.
- **Authorities** when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
- **Successors** in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified if required by law).

We do **not** publish a public list of every vendor or internal system we use. Our providers may change as the product evolves.

## 6. AI and automated processing

Bookfellow uses automated and AI-assisted processing to generate companion material, answer questions about **books you have unlocked**, organize notes, and similar features.

- Processing is tied to **your account, your titles, and your marked progress**.
- We design features so companion content does not spoil material you have not reached yet.
- **Do not submit sensitive personal information** (health, financial account numbers, government IDs, etc.) in chat, notes, or voice features unless you choose to — we are not designed to collect it.

## 7. Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above, including:

- **Account data** — while your account is active and for a reasonable period after closure (for backups, disputes, and legal compliance).
- **Billing records** — as required for tax and accounting.
- **Support email** — as long as needed to resolve your request and maintain a reasonable support history.
- **Waitlist email** — until you unsubscribe, we remove you, or the waitlist is retired.

We may retain aggregated or de-identified data longer.

## 8. Cookies and analytics

We may use **essential cookies** required for security and basic operation.

When we enable **analytics** or marketing tools, we will use cookies or similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site. Where required, we will provide choices (for example, a cookie notice or consent mechanism). You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

## 9. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- **access** a copy of personal information we hold about you;
- **correct** inaccurate information;
- **delete** certain information or close your account;
- **opt out** of marketing emails (every marketing email will include an unsubscribe link);
- **limit** certain processing (where applicable law provides).

To exercise these rights, email **[support@bookfellow.io](mailto:support@bookfellow.io)**. We may need to verify your identity. We will not discriminate against you for exercising lawful privacy rights.

**California residents:** We do not sell personal information. You may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA; contact us to submit a request.

**Other U.S. states:** Similar state privacy laws may apply; contact us with your state and request.

## 10. Children

Bookfellow is **not directed to children under 13**, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

## 11. United States

Bookfellow is a **United States** company. Our service is offered to customers in the **United States**. Information is processed in the United States.

## 12. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 13. Third-party links

The site may link to third-party sites (for example, app stores or publisher pages). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this one.

## 14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on **bookfellow.io** with a new “Last updated” date. Material changes may also be communicated by email or in-product notice. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

## 15. Contact

**Bookfellow**  
Email: **[support@bookfellow.io](mailto:support@bookfellow.io)**  
Web: **https://bookfellow.io**
