Turn Bookfellow into a **publisher-aligned attach** to owned books: free companions with purchase, measurable redeem attribution, co-marketing, and (later) placement — without becoming a substitute library.

Public **Partner with us** is one surface of this plan, not the plan itself.

## Billable event — locked (Brian 2026-07-22)

**Lean:** When a redeem code is **entered**, we **auto-assign the book** to the account. Code entered and companion unlocked are **the same event**.

Product implication for www: do not treat “pending code” and “fulfilled unlock” as two partner-billable moments — settle on the single enter/assign. (Internal plumbing may still stage pending→fulfill; commercially it’s one count.)

Still open:

1. **Unit:** $/redeem · % of book · free-with-purchase only (publisher pays gen) · rev-share?
2. **Who invoices whom:** Bookfellow → publisher · publisher → Bookfellow · net settle?
