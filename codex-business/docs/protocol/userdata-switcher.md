# Userdata Switcher — Model A (daily use)

**Extension:** Userdata Switcher `1.4.1` (`domoarigatomrburato.userdata-switcher`).

## Daily

1. Open **Cursor.app** (one Dock pin).
2. `Cmd+Shift+U` (or status bar) → **Work** / **Personal** / **Ventures**.
3. Confirm Settings → Account email matches the seat.

## Ventures after Build

Open workspace **`/mnt/DataStore/Ventures`** (root). Confirm `cursor-shared/` and `project-codex/` visible.

## Do not

- Pin secondary Cursor windows to the Dock (dead icons).
- Use Account Switcher or Seat Swap for daily dual-seat.
- Sign the wrong seat into the wrong email.
